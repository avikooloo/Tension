TENSION

THE BEAUTIFUL TENSION

is when there is perfect conflict between the "Product truth" and "Culture truth"

1. THE QUESTION

For tension to be achieved certain ‘questions’ need to be answered

2. THE CHALLANGE

What is the most relevant and differentiating idea that will surprise consumers or challenge their current thinking of the brand?

3. THE CONFLICT

What is the psychological, social or cultural tension associated with this idea? What makes our target tense about the idea?

4. THE TALK VALUE

What about the brand could help us start a dialogue between the brand and the consumers/target?
